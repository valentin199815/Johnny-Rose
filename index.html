Who are we?

Johnny and The Rose have been performing together for over 40 years in various bands.

Their family band,The Molnars, were awarded BCCMA’S “Group of the Year” in 2001.

Their Original music has also been used in the film industry.

This husband and wife duo play a mix of Country, Pop, Rock, Blues and Originals
